FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PetLog free?+

Yes, PetLog is completely free. No account sign-up needed — just download and start. All data is stored only on your device.

Does it work without internet?+

Yes, PetLog works fully offline. There is no server — all data is stored in a local database on your device.

What health records can I track?+

You can track four types: weight, vet visits, medications, and vaccinations. Photos can be attached to each record, and you can set reminder notifications.

How does walk logging work?+

Tap Start Walk to begin and End Walk when you're done. You can take up to 5 photos during the walk. GPS location is saved automatically and shown on a mini-map.

What is the Monthly Recap?+

Monthly Recap automatically summarizes your pet's walks, photos, and health records for the month — a delightful way to look back on time spent together.

Is my data safe?+

PetLog does not use any server. All data is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted externally.
